Omelette Recipe


Description

This is a mushroom omelette recipe that I make pretty often. It requires very few ingredients, it's easy to make, and most importantly it's very delicious.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 eggs
  • Milk
  • Oil or Butter
  • Grated cheese
  • Mushrooms (sliced)

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan and melt butter in the pan or add oil to the pan
  2. In a separate pan add 2 handfuls of mushrooms evenly placed in the pan, and let it cook
  3. Crack 3-4 eggs in a small bowl and whisk thoroughly
  4. Add a small amount of milk to the egg mix and whisk further until fully mixed
  5. Add the eggs to the pan and let it sit until there is a cooked layer underneath
  6. Fold the omelette half way, and add grated cheese inside
  7. Once the mushrooms are fully golden brown then add them on top of the cheese inside the omelette
  8. Fold the omelette once again until the mushrooms and the cheese are fully wrapped
  9. Let the omelette cook until you reach the desired consistency (runny vs well cooked)
  10. Carefully place the omelette in a plate, and enjoy.
