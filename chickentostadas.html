Chicken Tostadas Recipe

Description

This is a classic mexican dish, that is easy to make and contains a fair amount of protein and fiber, which puts this dish on the healthier side.

Ingredients

  • Tostadas
  • Beans
  • Chicken breast
  • Grated Cheese
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken until fully cooked
  2. Let it cool and cut up the chicken into many bits
  3. Heat up some beans from a can, and spread them onto a tostada
  4. Add cheese on top of the beans
  5. then add a handful of chicken to each tostada
  6. Finally, slice some avocado and add them evenly on top of the chicken
